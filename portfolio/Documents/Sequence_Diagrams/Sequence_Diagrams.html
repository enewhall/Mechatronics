1.1 Move part from separator to camera

Initial Conditions

  • Part is in hopper
  • Vibration motor is off
  • Conveyer belt is stationary

Final Conditions

  • Part is in frint of camera
  • Vibration motor is off
  • Conveyer belt is stationary

Sequence Diagram


2.1 Pick up part from camera (correct orientation)

Initial Conditions

  • Part is front of camera in correct orientation
  • Magnet for part placer is off
  • Flipper Magnet is on
  • Part placer located over tray
  • Placer arm is retracted
  • Flipper in neutral position

Final Conditions

  • Magnet for part placer is on
  • Flipper Magnet is on
  • Part placer located over camera
  • Placer arm is retracted
  • Flipper in neutral position

3.1 Place part on tray

Initial Conditions

  • part is being held by magnet
  • part is correct orientation
  • linear actuator is retracted

Final Conditions

  • magnet is off
  • part is on tray
  • linear actuator is retracted

Sequence Diagram


4.1 Place flux on part

Initial Conditions

  • Tray is in front of part placer
  • Part has no flux or wire on it

Final Conditions

  • Tray is in front of flux and wire dispensor
  • part has flux and needs wire
