Servo Motor:

Servo pin: 9,10

DC Motors:

Motor Pin A: 46-48
Motor Pin B: 50-52

Encoders for DC Motors:

Encoder Pin A: 2, 21, 19
Encoder Pin B: 3, 20, 18

Stepper Motors:

Step Pin:	25-29
Enable Pin:	30-34
Direction Pin:	35-39

pins for other part needed(like electromagnet and limit switches)
