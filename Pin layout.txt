Servo Motor:

Servo pin: 9,10


Encoders:

Encoder Pin A: 2, 21, 19
Encoder Pin B: 3, 20, 18

Stepper Motors:

Step Pin:	25-29
Enable Pin:	30-34
Direction Pin:	35-39

DC Motors with Encoders:

Motor Pin A: 40, 41, 42
Motor Pin B: 43, 44, 45

DC Motors with no Encoders(hopefully none):

Motor Pin A: 46-49
Motor Pin B: 50-53
